Frequently Asked Questions

How can I submit a recipe?

You can submit your recipe through our "Post Your Blog" page. No registration required! Just fill out the form with your recipe details and we'll review it for publication.

How long does recipe approval take?

We typically review and approve recipes within 2-3 business days. You'll receive an email notification once your recipe is published.

Can I edit my submitted recipe?

Yes! Contact us with your recipe title and the changes you'd like to make. We'll update it for you.

Do you accept recipes from other regions?

While we focus on Jammu & Kashmir cuisine, we welcome traditional recipes from all regions that have cultural significance and authentic preparation methods.
