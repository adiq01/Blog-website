About Duggarswad

Preserving the culinary heritage of Jammu & Kashmir

Our Story

Duggarswad was born from a deep love for the traditional cuisine of Jammu and Kashmir. As we watched our grandmothers' recipes slowly fade from memory and our traditional cooking methods give way to modern convenience, we realized the urgent need to preserve these culinary treasures.

The name "Duggarswad" combines "Duggar" (referring to the Dogra people and region) with "swad" (meaning taste or flavor in Hindi). It represents our mission to capture and share the authentic taste of our homeland.

Every recipe shared here carries with it the warmth of family kitchens, the joy of festival preparations, and the love that goes into feeding our families. We believe that food is not just sustenance â€“ it's culture, memory, and identity.

Our Mission

Preserve Heritage

Document and preserve traditional recipes that are at risk of being forgotten, ensuring they pass to future generations.

Build Community

Create a platform where people can share their family recipes and connect over their love for traditional cuisine.

Share Knowledge

Make traditional cooking techniques and recipes accessible to everyone, regardless of their location or background.

What We Stand For

Authenticity

We prioritize genuine, traditional recipes over modern adaptations, ensuring the true essence of Dogra cuisine is preserved.

Community

Every recipe comes with a story, connecting us to the people and traditions that make our cuisine special.

Accessibility

We make it easy for anyone to contribute and learn, breaking down barriers to sharing culinary knowledge.

Quality

Each recipe is carefully reviewed to ensure accuracy and authenticity before being shared with our community.
